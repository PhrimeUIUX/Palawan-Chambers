Vision
A united and progressive MIMAROPA region where every island province is economically connected, business-friendly, and globally competitive — enabling communities and enterprises to thrive in sustainable growth.
Mission
To strengthen inter-island collaboration and promote inclusive economic development by: Connecting chambers, industries, and local governments across the island provinces; Empowering MSMEs and enterprises to grow through innovation, capacity building, and partnerships; Advocating policies and programs that foster ease of doing business, investment, and sustainable tourism; and Ensuring that every business in the region thrives and contributes to a resilient, inclusive economy.
